This folder contains papers for the project **Refining LLM Trading**.

## Priority Papers from Paper-Finder (relevance >= 2)

1. [StockBench: Can LLM Agents Trade Stocks Profitably In Real-world Markets?](2510.02209v1_StockBench_Can_LLM_Agents_Trade_Stocks_Profitably_In_Real_world_Market.pdf)
- Authors: Yanxu Chen et al.
- Year: 2025
- arXiv: 2510.02209
- Why relevant: Direct benchmark for multi-month sequential LLM trading and risk-adjusted metrics.

2. [FinPos: A Position-Aware Trading Agent System for Real Financial Markets](2510.27251v2_FinPos_A_Position_Aware_Trading_Agent_System_for_Real_Financial_Market.pdf)
- Authors: Bijia Liu, Ronghao Dang
- Year: 2025
- arXiv: 2510.27251
- Why relevant: Explicitly proposes position-aware, longer-horizon decision making instead of one-step trade classification.

3. [Hierarchical Organization Simulacra in the Investment Sector](2410.00354v1_Hierarchical_Organization_Simulacra_in_the_Investment_Sector.pdf)
- Authors: Chung-Chi Chen et al.
- Year: 2024
- arXiv: 2410.00354
- Why relevant: Studies multi-agent investment roles over long time horizons and compares with professional decisions.

4. [Can LLM-based Financial Investing Strategies Outperform the Market in Long Run?](2505.07078v5_Can_LLM_based_Financial_Investing_Strategies_Outperform_the_Market_in_.pdf)

- arXiv: 2505.07078

5. [HedgeAgents: A Balanced-aware Multi-agent Financial Trading System](2502.13165v1_HedgeAgents_A_Balanced_aware_Multi_agent_Financial_Trading_System.pdf)
- Authors: Xiangyu Li et al.
- Year: 2025
- arXiv: 2502.13165
- Why relevant: Shows multi-agent risk-balancing and hedging approaches for volatile regimes.

6. [Look-Ahead-Bench: a Standardized Benchmark of Look-ahead Bias in Point-in-Time LLMs for Finance](2601.13770v1_Look_Ahead_Bench_a_Standardized_Benchmark_of_Look_ahead_Bias_in_Point_.pdf)

- Year: 2026
- arXiv: 2601.13770

7. [Behavioral Consistency Validation for LLM Agents](2602.07023v1_Behavioral_Consistency_Validation_for_LLM_Agents_An_Analysis_of_Tradin.pdf)
